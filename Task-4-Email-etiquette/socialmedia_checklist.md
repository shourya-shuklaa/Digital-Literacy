# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do's
* *Do* curate a professional digital identity on platforms like LinkedIn or GitHub to showcase your academic and technical projects.
* *Do* pause and think critically before sharing or reposting information to ensure it is accurate and appropriate.
* *Do* use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for all your accounts.
* *Do* review and update your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your personal content.
* *Do* verify the credibility of news and links before clicking or sharing them with your network.

## Don'ts
* *Don't* post or share sensitive personal information (like your address, phone number, or student ID) publicly.
* *Don't* rely on informal or casual language when using social media for academic or professional networking.
* *Don't* engage in heated arguments or post emotionally charged content that could permanently damage your online reputation.
* *Don't* click on suspicious links, urgency-driven messages, or unsolicited internship offers, even if they appear to be from trusted sources.
* *Don't* assume that deleting a post removes it permanently from the internet; digital footprints are often permanent.
